We've decided to close the GoReleaser Discord server.

<!--more-->

Over time, the server has seen little real activity - most of what comes
through these days is spam, and genuine questions are few and far between.
Keeping it around and moderated is no longer a good use of our time.

Going forward, please ask questions, share ideas, and start conversations on
[GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/goreleaser/goreleaser/discussions)
instead.
It's easier to search, easier to keep track of, and keeps everything in one
place alongside the project.

Thanks to everyone who hung out with us on Discord over the years. 🙏

The server will remain there for a while, but previous invites no longer work.
